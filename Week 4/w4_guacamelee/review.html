Review of the Game Guacamelee!

Latoya Hall February 5, 2024

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Guacamelee! is a fun game for all ages. The nods to old school games like Metroid and Super Mario Brothers appeals to the older person in me.In fact, one battle ends with X'tabay showing up and saying "Nice try, but your princess is in another castle!" The fighting aspect mixed with a good story keeps you physically interested and mentally interested as well. The beautiful colors and dynamic characters make this game fun to play multiple times over!

This limited collector's box includes a flash-drive with a DRM-free game file, official soundtrack, instruction manual, Steam key, and various custom-made collectibles.[15] In August 2017, Vblank Entertainment released a limited retail version of Guacamelee! Super Turbo Championship Edition for the PlayStation 4. This release was limited to 3,800 copies.
  • The soundtrack is inspired by Mexican music that is composed with electronic beats, colorful synths, and EDM inspiration.
